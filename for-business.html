How can we help?

What is EventBeds™, and how can it benefit my event website?
EventBeds™ is a platform that allows you to offer exclusive accommodation to your event attendees. It provides a seamless booking experience on your website, generating revenue for your event while enhancing the attendee experience.
How can I integrate EventBeds™ into my event website?
Integrating the EventBeds™ platform is straightforward. Our technical team will provide you with the necessary HTML code to embed the booking widget on your website.
Do I need technical expertise to integrate EventBeds™ into my website?
Basic web development skills are helpful but not required. Our support team will guide you through the integration process, ensuring a smooth setup.
How does EventBeds™ generate revenue for my event?
EventBeds™ earns a commission from hotels for bookings made through your website. You receive a share of this commission, providing an additional revenue stream for your event.
Are there any costs associated with integrating EventBeds™ into my website?
There are no upfront costs for integrating EventBeds™. We earn a commission from hotel bookings, and you receive a share of that commission.
What types of accommodation can my attendee'sbook through EventBeds™?
EventBeds™ offers a wide range of accommodation, from budget-friendly to luxury options, ensuring that your staff, exhibitors and attendees can find suitable lodging.
Is customer support available for event organisers using EventBeds™?
Absolutely! EventBeds™ offers dedicated support to assist event organisers with any questions, issues, or customisation requests.
Is my attendees' personal information secure when booking through EventBeds™ on my website?
Yes, EventBeds™ follows strict privacy and security protocols to safeguard personal information.
